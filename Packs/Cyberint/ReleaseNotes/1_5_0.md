
#### Integrations

##### Check Point EM Feed

- Added the following commands:
	- ***cyberint-cve-enrich*** - Enriches one or more CVE identifiers with Cyberint vulnerability intelligence (EPSS, CVSS, CWE, the Cyberint CVE score and active-exploitation evidence).
	- ***cyberint-credential-leak-lookup*** - Looks up leaked employee or customer credentials associated with a company domain.

#### Playbooks

##### New: Cyberint - IOC Enrichment and Triage

- New: Enriches IOC entities (IP, domain, URL, file hash) found in an incident with Cyberint threat intelligence and applies triage decision logic, escalating the incident when a malicious indicator is found (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).

##### New: Cyberint - Credential Leak Validation and Response

- New: Validates and responds to leaked employee or customer credentials reported by Cyberint Argos, driving an automated or semi-automated remediation flow followed by user and SOC notification (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).

##### New: Cyberint - Vulnerability Exploitation Monitoring

- New: Enriches CVEs from a vulnerability-management incident with Cyberint vulnerability intelligence and prioritizes them based on real-world exploitation (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).

##### New: Cyberint - Phishing Takedown

- New: Automates or semi-automates the takedown of high-confidence phishing websites detected by Cyberint, including takedown submission and status polling (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).
