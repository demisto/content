#### Integrations

##### Cyberint Feed

Added support for the following commands:
- **cyberint-get-url** that gets url enrichment from the feed.
- **cyberint-get-file-sha256** that gets file sha256 enrichment from the feed.
- **cyberint-get-ipv4** that gets ipv4 enrichment from the feed.
- **cyberint-get-domain** that gets domain enrichment from the feed.
