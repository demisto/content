
#### Integrations

##### Cloudflare WAF

- Added 5 new commands for managing Cloudflare Rulesets:
  - ***cloudflare-waf-ruleset-list*** - List all rulesets at the account or zone level.
  - ***cloudflare-waf-ruleset-get*** - Get a specific ruleset by ID, including its list of rules.
  - ***cloudflare-waf-ruleset-create*** - Create a new ruleset at the account or zone level.
  - ***cloudflare-waf-ruleset-update*** - Update an existing ruleset by ID.
  - ***cloudflare-waf-ruleset-delete*** - Delete a ruleset by ID.
  
- Updated the Docker image to: *demisto/python3:3.12.13.10404775*.
