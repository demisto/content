# FireCompass

FireCompass is an Attack Surface Management (ASM) platform that continuously discovers, monitors, and prioritizes external attack surface risks. It identifies exposed assets such as domains, subdomains, IP addresses, web applications, and leaked credentials, helping organizations understand and reduce their external attack surface.

## What does this pack do?

This pack enables you to:

- Fetch risk events from the FireCompass platform for analysis in Cortex.
- Monitor external attack surface risks including takeover risks, DNS issues, and exposed services.
- Track risk lifecycle with entry status indicators for new and modified risks.
