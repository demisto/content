##### Zoom Dev
- Added the OAuth 2.0 authentication method.
- Added the following commands:
   - ***meeting-get***
   - ***meeting-list***
- Added a third meeting type - recurring meeting with fixed time
- Added the following arguments to the ***zoom-create-meeting*** command:
   - *end_date_time* - the final date on which the meeting will recur before it is canceled.
   - *end_times* - the number of times the meeting should recur before it is canceled. 
   - *recurrence_type* - the recurring meeting type.
   - *monthly_day* - the day of the month for the recurring meeting.
   - *monthly_week* - the week in the month for the recurring meeting.
   - *monthly_week_day* - the day in that specific week in the month for the recurring meeting.
   - *weekly_days* - the day in that specific week for the recurring meeting.
- Documentation and metadata improvements.
