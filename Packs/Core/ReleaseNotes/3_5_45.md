#### Integrations

##### Cortex Platform Core

- Updated the **core-search-assets** command to fix API_500 errors caused by invalid asset type filtering:
  - Switched from `EQ` to `CONTAINS` operator for all asset types.
  - Switched to `WILDCARD` operator (with a trailing `*` pattern) for *Server* (to avoid matching *Serverless*) and *Kubernetes Cluster* (to avoid matching *Kubernetes ClusterRole* and *Kubernetes ClusterRoleBinding*).
