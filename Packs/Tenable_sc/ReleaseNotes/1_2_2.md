
#### Integrations

##### Tenable.sc

- Improved vulnerability ingestion to generate a deterministic `hostUUID` and `vulnUUID` when they are not provided by Tenable.sc. The host identifier is derived from `repositoryID`, `ip`, and `dnsName`, and the vulnerability identifier is derived from `repositoryID`, `ip`, `port`, `protocol`, and `pluginID`, so findings can be correlated even when the native identifiers are missing.
