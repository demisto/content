##### Tenable.sc
- Added the **time_zone**, **start_time**, **repeat_rule_freq**, **repeat_rule_interval**, **repeat_rule_by_day**, **enabled** arguments to the **tenable-sc-create-scan** command. For more information, refer to the integration readme.

- Added the following commands: **tenable-sc-list-groups**, **tenable-sc-create-user**, **tenable-sc-update-user**, **tenable-sc-delete-user**, **tenable-sc-list-plugin-family**, **tenable-sc-create-policy**, **enable-sc-list-query**, **tenable-sc-launch-scan-report**, **tenable-sc-create-remediation-scan**, **tenable-sc-update-asset**.
