
#### Integrations

##### CrowdStrike Falcon

- Added the *mobile_action* argument to the ***cs-falcon-upload-custom-ioc*** command, allowing users to specify mobile-specific actions (no_action, allow, detect, prevent) when uploading custom IOCs.
- Added the *lift_filesystem_containment_all* argument to the ***cs-falcon-lift-host-containment*** command, enabling users to lift filesystem containment from hosts in addition to network containment.
