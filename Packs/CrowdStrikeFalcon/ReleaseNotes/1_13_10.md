
#### Integrations

##### CrowdStrike Falcon

- Added a new parameter **Reopen Statuses** to the integration configuration. It will represent the Crowdsrike Falcon statuses that will reopen an incident in XSOAR if it is closed.
- Added the fields **id** and **mobile_detection_id** to the mirroring process of **CrowdStrike Falcon IDP Detection** and **CrowdStrike Falcon Mobile Detection** respectively.
