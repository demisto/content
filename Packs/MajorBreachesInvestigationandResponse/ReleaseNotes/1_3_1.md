
#### Playbooks
##### New: HAFNIUM - Exchange 0-day exploits
- This playbook does the following:
Collect indicators to aid in your threat hunting process
 - Retrieve IOCs of HAFNIUM attack (Exchange 0-day exploits) 
 - Discover IOCs of associated activity related to the attack 
 - Generate an indicator list to block indicators with HAFNIUM tags
 - Query firewall logs to detect network activity 
 - Search endpoint logs for hashes to detect presence on hosts (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.5.0).
