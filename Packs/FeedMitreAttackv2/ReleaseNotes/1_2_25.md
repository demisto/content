#### Integrations

##### MITRE ATT&CK Feed v2

- Fixed an issue where the integration failed with `Exception: 'Stealth'` when MITRE ATT&CK introduced a new tactic (e.g., *Stealth*) that is referenced in attack patterns' kill-chain phases but whose `x-mitre-tactic` object has not yet been published to the TAXII feed. The integration now dynamically fetches the missing tactic from the TAXII server by its shortname and correctly builds the tactic relationship. If the tactic cannot be resolved, the relationship is skipped gracefully instead of raising an unhandled exception.
- Added *Stealth* to the kill-chain phases mapping.
