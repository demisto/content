#### Integrations

##### Unit 42 Intelligence

- Fixed an issue in the ***url*** command where URLs wrapped in double-quote or single-quote characters (e.g. `"url1","url2"` or `'url1','url2'`) were not correctly split and the surrounding quotes were sent to the API, causing a 400 error.
- Fixed an issue in the ***url*** command where URL fragments were not stripped before encoding, causing a 400 error for URLs containing `#` characters (e.g. `example.com/page#section`).
