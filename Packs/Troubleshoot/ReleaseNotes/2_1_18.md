
#### Scripts

##### New: TroubleshootRestartFailedPlaybooks

New: Scans all non-closed alerts for playbook tasks in Error state and restarts them from the specific failed task. The script reopens each errored task and re-executes it, allowing the playbook to continue from where it stopped. Includes throttling support via `group_size` and `sleep_time` arguments to avoid overwhelming the system (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.0.0).
