#### Integrations

##### DFIRe

- Replaced the catch-all `body` argument on `dfire-case-update-report` with explicit `report_id` and `report_text` arguments. The OpenAPI schema for `/cases/{id}/update-report/` advertises a generic body, but the handler actually requires `{id, report_text}` — playbook authors now get a tidy interface instead of having to hand-craft JSON.
