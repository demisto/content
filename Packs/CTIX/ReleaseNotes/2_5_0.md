#### Integrations

##### Cyware Intel Exchange

- Fixed an issue where the CTIX **is_revoked** flag was not mapped to the **Is Revoked** indicator field, making revoked indicators invisible to Threat Intel queries.
- Cleaned up the instance configuration page: removed the duplicate **Classifier** and **Mapper (incoming)** controls (the platform renders these automatically for a fetching integration), grouped indicator-feed settings under **Connect** and incident-fetch settings under **Collect**, and moved rarely used parameters (**Timeout**, **Bypass exclusion list**, **Tags**) to the Advanced Settings area. No parameter names or defaults changed.

#### Scripts

##### New: CTIXDeleteFlaggedIndicators

- New: Deletes indicators ingested from Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX v3) that are flagged as deprecated, revoked, false positive, or whitelisted. All delete flags are disabled by default.

#### Playbooks

##### New: CTIX - Delete Flagged Indicators

- New: Runs the CTIXDeleteFlaggedIndicators script with the configured delete flags. Intended to be triggered by the bundled job of the same name.

#### Jobs

##### New: CTIX - Delete Flagged Indicators

- New: Scheduled job (every 12 hours by default) that runs the 'CTIX - Delete Flagged Indicators' playbook. Deletes nothing until delete flags are enabled in the playbook inputs.

#### Indicator Fields

##### New: Is Deprecated

- New: Indicates whether the indicator is marked as deprecated in Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX).

##### New: Is False Positive

- New: Indicates whether the indicator is marked as a false positive in Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX).

##### New: Is Reviewed

- New: Indicates whether the indicator has been reviewed in Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX).

##### New: Is Revoked

- New: Indicates whether the source revoked the indicator in Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX).

##### New: Is Whitelisted

- New: Indicates whether the indicator is allow-listed in Cyware Intel Exchange (CTIX).
