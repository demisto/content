# ZeroFox

ZeroFOX, a leading cybersecurity firm specializing in digital risk protection, enhances the platform's capability to monitor and counter threats from various online platforms including social media, mobile apps, and the broader web. Through this integration, users can leverage ZeroFOX's expertise in identifying threats like phishing attacks, information leaks, and account compromises, all within the unified Cortex XSOAR environment, ensuring a holistic and agile approach to digital security

### What does this pack do?

- Mirror incidents between XSOAR incidents and ZeroFox alerts.
- Allow to request takedowns over threats.
- Allow to submit threats.
- Look up compromised assets in our CTI Feeds
