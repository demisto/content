
#### Integrations

##### New: O365 Message Trace

- New: Added the **O365 Message Trace** integration, which ingests Exchange Online Message Trace events into Cortex XSIAM. Message Trace enables tenant administrators to track the lifecycle of an email, determine its delivery status (delivered, pending, failed, or quarantined), and understand the actions applied to it.

- Added the following commands:
	- ***o365-message-trace-generate-login-url***
	- ***o365-message-trace-auth-test***
	- ***o365-message-trace-auth-reset***
	- ***o365-message-trace-get-events***
