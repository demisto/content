#### Integrations

##### Atlassian Confluence Cloud
- Added support for OAuth 2.0 authentication.
- Added the **Cloud ID**, **Client ID**, **Client Secret**, and **Callback URL** parameters to support OAuth 2.0.
- Added the `confluence-cloud-oauth-start`, `confluence-cloud-oauth-complete`, and `confluence-cloud-oauth-test` commands to manage the OAuth 2.0 authorization flow.
