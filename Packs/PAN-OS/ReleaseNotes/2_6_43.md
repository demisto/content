
#### Integrations

##### Panorama

- Fixed an issue where the incident **occurred** time was offset from the **created** time when the firewall/Panorama was configured in a non-UTC time zone, because naive log timestamps (such as *time_generated* and *match_time*) were mislabeled as UTC.
- Added a new *Firewall/Panorama Time Zone* parameter, used to convert fetched log timestamps to UTC so the incident **occurred** time is correct. Defaults to *UTC* to preserve the previous behavior.
