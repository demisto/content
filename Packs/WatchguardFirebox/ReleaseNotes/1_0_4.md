
#### Modeling Rules

##### Watchguard Firebox Private Access Modeling Rule

Fixed an issue where the modeling rule inverted the source/target zone and sent bytes mappings. Inbound interfaces (**in_if**) are now mapped to **xdm.source.zone**, outbound interfaces (**out_if**) to **xdm.target.zone**, and the **sent_bytes**/**rcvd_bytes** values are mapped to the correct **xdm.source.sent_bytes**/**xdm.target.sent_bytes** fields. This resolves false positive "Large Uploads" analytics alerts caused by download traffic being misclassified as uploads.
