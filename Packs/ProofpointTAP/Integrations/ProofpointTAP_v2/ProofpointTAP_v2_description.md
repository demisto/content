To configure an instance of the integration in Cortex XSOAR, you need to supply your Service Principal and Service Secret. <br>
When you configure the integration instance, enter the Service Principal in the Service Principal field, and the Service Secret in the Password field.

To create the credentials, see the Generate TAP Service Credentials section in 
1. Log in to the TAP dashboard at https://threatinsight.proofpoint.com.
2. Navigate to **Settings > Connected Applications**.
3. Click **Create New Credential**.
4. Name the new credential set and click **Generate**.
