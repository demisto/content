The XSOAR integration with Cisco Umbrella allows users of XSOAR to undertake automated actions inside of Umbrella Reporting v2 as part of their security operations. Cisco Umbrella is a cloud security and threat intelligence platform that provides protection and visibility against threats on the internet wherever users go.

##### What does this pack do?
This pack allows Cisco Umbrella users to automate Reporting tasks such as fetch summary of activities, list top threats and more increasing their efficiency and saving valuable time.
- Get a summary of top identities, destinations, URLs, categories, threats, threat types, events and IPs being observed in your environment within a specific timeframe.
- Get a list of active threats being observed in your environment within a specific timeframe (includes DNS and Proxy data).
