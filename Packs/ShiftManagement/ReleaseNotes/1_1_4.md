
#### Scripts
##### New: OutOfOffice
- Add/Remove yourself from the out of office list in XSOAR.

When used with the SimpleAssignAnalystToIncident automation, prevents Incidents from being assigned to you while out of office. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.5.0).
##### New: AssignAnalystToIncidentOOO
- Assign analyst to incident with consideration of PTO, OOO, or Disabled Users.

Users can use the OutOfOffice automation to add or remove themselves from the list. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.5.0).
##### New: AssignToNextShiftOOO
- Randomly assigns the incidents to users on call (requires shift management) and users on call. This automation works with the other Out of Office automations to ensure only available users get assigned.

Incident Ids should be passed in as a comma separated list. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.5.0).
##### New: OutOfOfficeListCleanup
- Cleans the out of office list for XSOAR users, removing any where the off until day is in the past.   Can be used with the JOB - Out of Office Cleanup Example playbook. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 5.5.0).
