##### New: Safebreach

- New: SafeBreach automatically executes thousands of breach methods from its extensive and growing Hacker’s Playbook™ to validate security control effectiveness. Simulations are automatically correlated with network, endpoint, and SIEM solutions providing data-driven SafeBreach Insights for holistic remediation to harden enterprise defenses.
 (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.2.0).
