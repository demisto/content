
#### Indicator Fields
- The following are new indicator fields:
   - **Confidence** 
   - **Vulnerabilities**
   - **Reports**
   - **Targets** 
- The following indicator fields are assigned to all indicator types:
   - **IP Address**
   - **Domain Name**
   - **STIX Kill Chain Phases**
   - **Updated Date** 
   - **Malware Family**
   - **Actor** 
   - **Creation Date**
