## Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP Content Pack
---

This content pack enables Cortex XSOAR to integrate with Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP and fetch DLP reports. This pack includes the **Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP** integration.


### Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP
---

Integrate with Enterprise DLP service to get details about DLP violations

The integration includes commands to:
 - Fetch DLP reports with data pattern match details.
 - Fetch DLP reports with data pattern match details and the corresponding snippet from the file.
