## Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP Content Pack
---

This content pack enables XSOAR to integrate with Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP. It includes following integration.


### Palo Alto Networks Enterprise DLP
---

Integrate with Enterprise DLP service to provide details DLP violation details

The integration includes commands to:
 - Fetch DLP reports with Data pattern match details
 - Fetch DLP reports with Data pattern match details and corresponding snippet from the file.
