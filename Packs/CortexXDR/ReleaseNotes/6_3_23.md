
#### Integrations

##### Palo Alto Networks Cortex XDR - Investigation and Response

- Added the *extra_data* argument to the ***xdr-list-cases*** command, allowing retrieval of cases with their extended data.
- Fixed an issue where the *limit* argument was ignored in the ***xdr-list-cases*** command. Manual pagination (*page*/*page_size*) and automatic pagination (*limit*) are now handled as distinct, non-overlapping mechanisms.
