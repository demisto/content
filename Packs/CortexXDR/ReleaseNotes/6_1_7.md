
#### Playbooks

##### New: Cortex XDR IOCs - Disable expired IOCs in XDR

- New: This playbook disables indicators in Cortex XDR after they have expired from Cortex XSOAR. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).
##### New: Cortex XDR IOCs - Push new IOCs to XDR (Main)

New: The playbook will "sync" IOCs into Cortex XDR by pushing new IOCs in and disabling expired IOCs. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).
##### New: Cortex XDR IOCs - Push new IOCs to XDR

New: Pushes new IOCs into Cortex XDR tagging them as `xdr_pushed`. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.10.0).
