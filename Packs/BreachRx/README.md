### What does this pack do?
Create and monitor the status of a BreachRx Incident directly from your existing Cortex XSOAR Incident, so your team can streamline collaboration across all the stakeholders in the organization, including security, legal, privacy, and communications, so they can manage the sensitive requirements for your Incident on the BreachRx platform, such as regulatory and contractual requirements, protect privilege, while your team can continue to manage the cybersecurity aspects of your Incident without having to leave the XSOAR platform.

### About BreachRx

BreachRx is the intelligent incident response platform that provides operational resilience for the entire enterprise. Its patented technology brings order to the chaos before, during, and after incidents by automatically generating tailored incident response plans and providing targeted guidance to relevant stakeholders through every step of the process. Integrated privileged communication channels and audit trails ensure compliance with rapidly evolving standards and proactively protect CISOs and executive leadership from personal liability.
