
#### Integrations

##### PAN-OS Policy Optimizer (Beta)

Added the `device_group` agrument to the *pan-os-po-app-and-usage*, *pan-os-po-get-rules*, *pan-os-po-get-stats*, *pan-os-po-no-apps*, *pan-os-po-unused-apps* command. This allows the Panorama device group to be specified when running these commands.
