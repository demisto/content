
#### Scripts
##### New: PcapConvert
Convert packet data to the standard pcap. Currently, it only supports CDL(NGFW) pcap from which to convert. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.2.0).

##### New: PcapFileExtractStreams
Extract payloads of each stream from a pcap file. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.2.0).

##### New: PcapExtractStreams
Extract payloads of each stream from a pcap. (Available from Cortex XSOAR 6.2.0).
