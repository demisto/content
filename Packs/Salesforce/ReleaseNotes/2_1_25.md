
#### Integrations

##### Salesforce

Added three remediation commands:
- ***salesforce-delete-file***: Soft-deletes a file (ContentDocument) to the Recycle Bin, cascading to its versions.
- ***salesforce-archive-knowledge-article***: Archives a Knowledge article (non-destructive), setting *PublishStatus* to *Archived* via the standard *archiveKnowledgeArticles* action.
- ***salesforce-remove-file-public-links***: Removes a file public link by deleting the *ContentDistribution* row.
