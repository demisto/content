# Mimecast

The Mimecast platform offers protection against email threats and data leaks while preventing service downtime through email archiving and uptime services.

## What does this pack do?

<~XSOAR>
Use the Mimecast v2 integration in the content pack to automate protection and remediation actions against email threats and fetch Impersonation, Attachments, URL, and Held Messages incidents from Mimecast.
</~XSOAR>

<~XSIAM>
Use the Mimecast Event Collector v2 in the content pack to fetch Audit and SIEM events from Mimecast.
</~XSIAM>

**Note**: All <~XSOAR>incident</~XSOAR> <~XSIAM>event</~XSIAM> timestamps are in the Coordinated Universal Time (UTC) timezone.
