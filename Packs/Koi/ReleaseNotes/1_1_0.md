
#### Integrations

##### KOI

- Added support for 12 new commands:
  - ***koi-policy-list*** - Retrieves a list of all policies.
  - ***koi-allowlist-get*** - Retrieves all items in the allowlist.
  - ***koi-allowlist-items-add*** - Adds items to the global allowlist.
  - ***koi-allowlist-items-remove*** - Removes items from the global allowlist.
  - ***koi-blocklist-get*** - Retrieves all items in the blocklist.
  - ***koi-blocklist-items-add*** - Adds items to the global blocklist.
  - ***koi-blocklist-items-remove*** - Removes items from the global blocklist.
  - ***koi-policy-status-update*** - Enables or disables a policy by ID.
  - ***koi-inventory-list*** - Lists inventory items with pagination and filtering.
  - ***koi-inventory-item-get*** - Retrieves details for a specific inventory item.
  - ***koi-inventory-search*** - Searches inventory items using advanced query builder filters.
  - ***koi-inventory-item-endpoints-list*** - Lists endpoints that have a specific inventory item installed.
