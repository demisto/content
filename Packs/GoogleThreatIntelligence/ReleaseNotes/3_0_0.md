#### Integrations

##### New: Google Threat Intelligence - Relevance System Alerts

- Added the **Google Threat Intelligence - Relevance System Alerts** integration, which enables fetching and managing RS Alerts from Google Threat Intelligence, with support for bi-directional mirroring in Cortex XSOAR and configurable alert filtering by relevance, severity, priority, status, and threat scenario.
- Added the following commands:
  - ***gti-rs-alert-list***: Lists RS Alerts with filtering by relevance, severity, priority, status, threat scenario, and time range.
  - ***gti-rs-alert-get***: Retrieves a specific RS Alert by ID.
  - ***gti-rs-alert-status-update***: Updates the status of an RS Alert.
