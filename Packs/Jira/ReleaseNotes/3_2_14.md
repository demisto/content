
#### Integrations

##### Atlassian Jira v3

- Added the **RawDescription** field to the Jira Issue fields. This field contains the raw description of the Jira issue, without any parsing. 
- The field **Ticket.RawDescription** is now available in ***fetch-incidents*** and in the following commands:
  - ***jira-issue-query***
  - ***jira-edit-issue***
  - ***jira-get-issue***
  - ***jira-issue-assign***
